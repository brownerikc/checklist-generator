Checklist Generator (Beta)

  1. Select your checklist and output types to get a customized checklist.
  2. Use the Print option in your web browser to print this checklist to a PDF.
    Note: You can omit printing page 1 to leave out these instructions and the generator tools.
  3. The Content Writer completes the CW column of the checklist.
    Note: This process may reveal that some parts of your document are not consistent with checklist expectations. Unless you have team- or product-specific exceptions, fix these items so that you can check them off.
  4. When complete, the Content Writer hands the checklist to the Lead Reviewer, who completes the LR column. The Lead Reviewer may find additional errors in the document and should inform the Content Writer.
  5. The Content Writer addresses the issues brought up by the Lead Reviewer.
  6. (Default) The Lead Reviewer completes the checklist. (Some writer/reviewer pairs may choose to complete this step at the end of step 4.)
  7. The Content Writer stores the checklist in the appropriate location (varies by team) and moves on to the Finalization Phase (localized products) or Publishing Phase (non-localized products).
